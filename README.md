#  Superstore Sales Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project features an **interactive Power BI dashboard** developed using the Superstore dataset to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and the impact of discounts.  
The dashboard is designed to help business users quickly identify **growth opportunities, profit leakages, and regional performance trends**.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales, profit, and order trends  
- Understand the impact of discount levels on profitability  
- Identify top and bottom performing product sub-categories  
- Compare regional and city-level performance  
- Highlight customer-level profitability patterns  

---

## Key KPIs
- **Total Sales:** $2M  
- **Total Profit:** $286K  
- **Total Orders:** 10K  
- **Average Profit Margin:** 12.46%

---

##  Dashboard Highlights

### Sales & Order Trends
- Monthly trend analysis shows **sales peaking in November**, driven by higher order volume.
- Consistent growth is observed during festive and year-end periods.

### Profitability & Discounts
- Discount ranges above **40% frequently result in losses**, despite high sales volume.
- Optimal profit margins are observed in **0–10% and 10–20% discount ranges**.

### Product Performance
- **Copiers, Phones, and Accessories** are the most profitable sub-categories.
- **Tables and Bookcases** generate losses, indicating pricing or cost inefficiencies.

### Regional Insights
- **West and East regions** contribute the highest revenue and profit.
- Central and South regions show comparatively lower profitability.

### City & Customer Analysis
- Some high-revenue cities display **negative profit margins**, highlighting discount or cost issues.
- Customer-level analysis reveals variability in profitability despite similar sales volumes.
